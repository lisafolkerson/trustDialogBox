How to Use

A simple and effective pop up box that does whatever it does bc Roger Federer was voted the second most trustworthy man on earth by Readers Digest Listeners.

Uses CSS animations to style an alert box to put you at ease with the reassuring face of someone you can trust.

To use, download files below, include in your project files and in your javascript, call the .trustDialogBox() function.
